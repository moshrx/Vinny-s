Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

Vinny's Coffee & Pizza ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information on our website and in our physical location in O'Leary, PEI.

2. Information We Collect

Website Data: This website is designed as a digital menu. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or any technology that identifies individual visitors. Your IP address is briefly processed by our hosting provider to deliver the site content but is not stored by us.

Direct Contact: If you choose to call us to place an order, we collect only the necessary details to complete your transaction, such as your name and phone number.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

Our website data is powered by Google Sheets. While we pull menu information from Google, no personal data about you is sent back to Google through our implementation. Our website is maintained by Redsoil Digital, which follows strict privacy-first development standards.

4. Data Security

We implement physical and digital safeguards to protect any information provided during phone orders. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

As a Prince Edward Island resident, you have the right to ask what information we have about you. Since we do not store digital accounts, this is typically limited to recent order history at our physical shop.
